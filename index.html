The Tillamook Getaway

Beaches, waterfalls & blue lakes from a single basecamp on the Bayocean peninsula — easy days, short drives, BBQ nights.

JUN 27 – JUL 1 Basecamp · 4510 Bayocean Rd NW, Tillamook OR Home · Coquitlam, BC
① Drive Down ② Three Capes ③ Cannon Beach ④ Falls & Lake ⑤ Drive Home Summary

Everything runs out of one rental west of Tillamook — no packing up between nights. The two travel days bookend three relaxed basecamp days, each kept within about an hour's drive each way.

The Museum of Flight has been dropped — Seattle was too far off-route to make the day work. Both driving days are now relaxed straight-shots.

Parking passes

Ecola is $12; Cape Kiwanda & Drift Creek run ~$5–10. Carry small bills, or buy a Drift Creek pass online / at BiMart in Lincoln City.

Tide tables

Check before Oceanside (the tunnel) and Short Beach — both are best and safest at lower tide.

No signal

Drift Creek Falls has no cell service. Download your directions before leaving the rental.

BBQ supplies

Tillamook Creamery (till 6pm) for cheese; the Fred Meyer in town for meat and groceries.

Pack a flashlight, chase the tide.
